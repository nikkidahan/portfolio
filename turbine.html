wind turbine project

Back in high school, I built a fully functioning wind turbine. This project was sponsored by the University of Vermont College of Engineering.The goal was to build a sustainable energy resource. My team chose to build a wind turbine because we considered it to be one of the most realistic energy resources for the future. We created multiple designs for the blades and tested many materials in order to determine which combination produced the most energy. After completing our wind turbine, we presented our results to the judges, and our team won the "Best Analysis Award!"
